In this figure I apply 200 Gt/yr signal to Iceland with a buffer of 0.5 and Guassian noise and then recover Iceland at a variety of different buffers.
I do not use any corrections (GIA, Elastic Response, Solid Earth, etc.).
Ellsemere Island is not accounted for.  Data spans April 2002 - June 2017.
